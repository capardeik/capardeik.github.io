    • Summary
    • Abbigail is the oldest (or youngest) daughter of John and Lucy Lazoo. She likes to spend her time tinkering with machines, inventing stuff, and doing the impossible. She has become the "main" character in many of the comics, as John has deemed her more interesting to write about than himself.
